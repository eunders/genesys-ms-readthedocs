1. Accessing PantaSys
=====================

To access the **PantaSys**, follow these steps:

1.1 Logging In
--------------

.. image:: /_static/en/login.jpg
    :alt: Optional alt text for the image
    :align: center
    :width: 300px

1. **Navigate to the Website**  
   Open your preferred web browser and go to the **PantaSys** website.

2. **Login**  
   Enter your credentials (**username** and **password**) in the login fields provided.

3. **Submit**  
   Click the **"Sign In"** button to access the dashboard.
